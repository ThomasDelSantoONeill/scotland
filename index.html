Deposit & Payment

Key financial terms from the Motorhome Escapes rental agreement

Damage Deposit / Deductible Refundable
£2,000

Pre-authorised on credit card 3 days before pickup. Not a charge — just a hold on the card. Lead driver must use their own credit card for the full amount.

Deposit Release Conditions
  • No damages: Hold released 7 days after rental ends, following vehicle inspection
  • Minor damage (no insurance claim): Repair costs deducted, remainder refunded to same card. Body shop estimates can take up to 14 days
  • Insurance claim raised: Full deposit retained toward the £2,000 insurance excess until claim is settled (can be a lengthy process)
  • Interior damage: Not covered by insurance — deducted from deposit at your expense
  • Second incident: A new £2,000 card payment required to continue the rental
International card fee: A 3% fee applies to all payments from non-UK bank cards (charged by Stripe, not Motorhome Escapes). Since we're paying from Spain, budget for this surcharge on the total hire cost and deposit.
